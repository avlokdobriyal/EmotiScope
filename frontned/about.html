About EmotiScope

EmotiScope is a powerful, AI-powered emotion detection system that analyzes textual input to identify emotional tone and predict moods and recommend you movies, tv shows and songs in real time.

Developed by students of GEHU Dehradun, it uses sentiment analysis and emotion classification to provide an intuitive interface for users to understand their feelings and then emotional mapping to recommend media based on that.
